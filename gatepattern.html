GATE Exam Pattern

Exam Details

Exam Mode: Online (Computer Based Test)

Duration: 3 hours

Total Marks: 100

Number of Questions: 65

Question Types

  • Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs): 1 mark or 2 marks
  • Multiple Select Questions (MSQs): 1 mark or 2 marks
  • Numerical Answer Type (NAT): 1 mark or 2 marks

Marking Scheme

  • MCQs: 1/3 mark deducted for every wrong answer in 1-mark MCQs and 2/3 mark deducted for every wrong answer in 2-mark MCQs.
  • MSQs and NATs: No negative marking.

Section Weights

  • General Aptitude (GA): 15 marks
  • Engineering Mathematics (EM): 15 marks
  • Subject Paper: 70 marks

The General Aptitude and Engineering Mathematics sections are compulsory for all candidates. The Subject Paper section will be based on the candidate's chosen subject.

Exam Preparation Tips

  • Start preparing early.
  • Make a study plan and stick to it.
  • Use good quality study materials.
  • Practice solving previous year question papers.
  • Take mock tests and analyze your performance.
  • Get enough sleep and eat healthy food on the day of the exam.
